# Anky — FAQ

_Last updated: 2026-04-15_

## What is Anky?

Anky is a writing practice. You write for 8 uninterrupted minutes, stream-of-consciousness, no backspace. What comes out is "an anky".

## Why 8 minutes?

Because that's roughly how long it takes for the inner critic to get tired and the real voice to show up.

## Why no backspace?

Editing while writing is the inner critic at work. Removing backspace removes the critic.

## What happens to my writing?

If you're anonymous on the web: nothing. It disappears when you close the tab.

If you're authenticated (iOS / Farcaster): your writing is encrypted on your device and sent sealed to our backend. We can't read it. Only the secure enclave on EC2 decrypts it, generates a reflection and image prompt, and returns those to us. See the [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy.md).

## What's a Mirror?

A Mirror is your seat in the current sojourn — a compressed NFT (cNFT) on Solana that proves you're a participant. There are 3,456 seats per sojourn. One per person.

## What's a sojourn?

A 96-day cycle, followed by 21 days of "Great Slumber", then the next sojourn starts. Sojourn 9 is currently active (2026-03-03 → 2026-06-07).

## What are the 8 kingdoms?

Each day in a sojourn belongs to one of 8 kingdoms (Primordia, Emblazion, Chryseos, Eleasis, Voxlumis, Insightia, Claridium, Poiesis), cycling every 8 days. The kingdom of the day flavors your anky.

## Do I need a wallet?

To mint a Mirror or save ankys long-term, yes. The phone (iOS app or Farcaster miniapp) holds the wallet. The web is just a writing surface.

## Is it free?

Writing is free. Optional features (gifts on the altar, premium features) cost money — paid via Stripe or USDC on Base.

## Is the AI judging me?

No. The reflection is a mirror, not a grade. There's no scoring of your writing.

## Is this on mainnet?

No. Currently Solana devnet. Mainnet is a future decision.
