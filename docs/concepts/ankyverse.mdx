# The Ankyverse

> Launch-scope note: for Sojourn 9, the public season structure is 12 regions of 8 days, for 96 total days. The 8 chakras/kingdoms remain an inner symbolic cycle and should not be described as the public season partition.

The Ankyverse is not a metaverse. It's not a game world. It's a mythological container — a structured symbolic space where human writing gets placed according to its energetic resonance.

When you write an anky, Claude reads it and determines which chakra it resonates with most strongly (1-8). That chakra maps to a kingdom. The kingdom maps to cities. Your writing's energy determines where in the Ankyverse your story lives.

## Structure

```
Ankyverse
├── 8 Chakras (energy centers)
│   ├── Each maps to 1 Kingdom
│   │   ├── Each Kingdom has cities
│   │   └── Each city has characters, landscapes, stories
│   └── Chakras/kingdoms are an inner symbolic cycle, not the Sojourn 9 public day partition
└── Anky (the narrator who lives inside every story)
```

## How Writing Maps to the Ankyverse

1. You write for 8+ minutes
2. Claude analyzes the chakra resonance of your writing
3. The resonance determines the kingdom
4. A story is generated set in that kingdom's landscape
5. Anky narrates the story from inside one of the characters
6. The story is translated into Spanish, Mandarin, Hindi, and Arabic
7. Images are generated for each story phase using Flux on the local GPU

The parent never needs to know which chakra their writing mapped to. The child never needs to know what the parent wrote. The Ankyverse is the translation layer between inner experience and shared narrative.

## Anky the Character

Anky is not an AI assistant. Anky is a narrator who exists inside the stories. In every cuentacuentos, Anky inhabits one of the characters and tells the story from within the narrative — not above it, not outside it.

This is important: Anky doesn't explain your writing to your child. Anky tells a story that carries the same energy as your writing, without ever breaking the fourth wall.
