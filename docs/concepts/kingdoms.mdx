# The Kingdoms

Each kingdom is a fully realized narrative world with its own geography, characters, culture, and visual language. When your writing resonates with a chakra, the cuentacuentos story is set in the corresponding kingdom.

## The Eight Kingdoms

### 1. Primordia (Root)
A vast underground network of caves, roots, and ancient stone. The beings here are builders and burrowers. Stories set in Primordia deal with foundations, safety, the body, and what it means to have a place in the world.

### 2. Emblazion (Sacral)
A world of rivers, hot springs, and shifting landscapes where creativity flows like water. The beings here are artists and shapeshifters. Stories deal with desire, creation, emotion, and the courage to feel.

### 3. Chryseos (Solar Plexus)
A sunlit realm of forges, arenas, and golden towers. The beings here are warriors and craftspeople. Stories deal with power, will, identity, and the fire that drives action.

### 4. Eleasis (Heart)
Vast forests, meadows, and healing springs where everything grows toward connection. The beings here are healers and bridge-builders. Stories deal with love, loss, grief, and the radical act of opening.

### 5. Voxlumis (Throat)
A realm where sound has physical form — crystalline structures built from spoken truth. The beings here are singers and truth-tellers. Stories deal with expression, silence, lies, and finding your voice.

### 6. Insightia (Third Eye)
A twilight realm of mirrors, libraries, and observatories. The beings here are seers and archivists. Stories deal with intuition, pattern recognition, dreams, and seeing what others miss.

### 7. Claridium (Crown)
An ethereal realm above the clouds where boundaries dissolve. The beings here are monks and mystics. Stories deal with transcendence, unity, surrender, and the dissolution of the self.

### 8. Poiesis (Soul Star)
The realm of creation itself — a workshop-world where all other kingdoms converge. The beings here are makers who work with the raw material of existence. Stories deal with integration, wholeness, and the mystery of being alive.

## Visual Language

Each kingdom has a distinct visual style used by the image generation pipeline:

- **Primordia**: Earth tones, cave systems, bioluminescence
- **Emblazion**: Warm watercolors, flowing forms, sunset palettes
- **Chryseos**: Gold and amber, sharp geometry, forge-light
- **Eleasis**: Deep greens, organic growth, dappled light
- **Voxlumis**: Crystal blues, sound waves made visible, resonant structures
- **Insightia**: Indigo and silver, reflective surfaces, starfields
- **Claridium**: White and violet, cloud formations, dissolving edges
- **Poiesis**: All colors converging, workshop aesthetics, raw creation

These visual cues are embedded in the image prompts generated by Ollama before being rendered by Flux/ComfyUI on the local GPU.
