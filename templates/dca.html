how this works

  1. minute timer triggers scripts/run_anky_dca.sh
  2. runner acquires a lock so jobs cannot overlap
  3. scripts/anky_dca_buy.py checks SOL balance and reserve guard
  4. if enough SOL, it requests quote+swap from Jupiter and submits on-chain tx
  5. all events are appended to logs/anky_dca.log
