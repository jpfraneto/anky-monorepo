👽 Dataset Round Two

Round one was a first attempt at teaching flux what anky looks like — we trained on ~112 hand-picked images and got a model that sort of understood the shape, the blue skin, the big ears, but still drifted. Round two is a larger, more intentional dataset: we generated hundreds of new ankys across different scenes and emotional registers, ran them through a tinder-style review to keep only the ones that felt right, and combined them with the original training images. The goal is a LoRA that doesn't need coaxing — one that holds anky's presence across wildly different prompts.
