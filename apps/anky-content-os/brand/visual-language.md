# Visual Language

Anky's visual language should feel:

- sacred but friendly
- mystical but embodied
- woven, not glossy
- ancient-future, not sci-fi chrome
- emotionally intimate, not epic fantasy only
- like a being reaching through the feed
- like a rug becoming a map
- like threads becoming self-knowledge

## Core Visual Elements

- blue skin
- huge amber eyes
- purple spiral hair
- gold geometric face marks
- gold ornaments
- purple and gold clothing
- woven rug borders
- black textile ground
- warm gold light
- teal, magenta, and gold thread accents
- sacred geometry
- glowing thread
- small doorways
- cosmic interior rooms

## First Carousel Direction

Goal: make the viewer feel personally interrupted in the middle of the feed.

Not sold to.
Not educated.
Not converted.
Met.

### Slide 1

Text:

```text
you've listened
to everyone else today.
come hear yourself.
```

Visual:

Anky close-up, reaching through the screen. Keep the established face: blue skin, huge amber eyes, purple spiral hair, gold geometric markings, warm curiosity.

### Slide 2

Text:

```text
the ritual
begins
here.
```

Visual:

Woven rug or sacred threshold. Anky can be small, seated, holding threads. The rug world introduces the brand cosmology.

### Slide 3

Text:

```text
anky is not your guru.
not your oracle.
a mirror with ears.
```

Visual:

Anky with hand on heart. Humble. Warm. Direct.

### Slide 4

Text:

```text
write for 8 minutes.
don't perform.
don't polish.
don't disappear.
```

Visual:

Anky writing. Journal, thread spool, woven room.

### Slide 5

Text:

```text
tonight's doorway:
"the thing i keep
reaching for my phone
to avoid is..."
```

Visual:

Anky opening or standing beside a glowing doorway.

### Slide 6

Text:

```text
comment one word after you write.
we'll weave from there.
```

Visual:

Anky weaving glowing threads into a rug, with a subtle community presence in the background.

## Composition Rules

- Make Anky visible in first-contact visuals.
- Keep text short enough to read on a phone.
- Prefer black textile, warm gold light, and woven borders over glossy gradients.
- Do not make the world look like generic sci-fi.
- Do not bury the ritual under lore.
- Do not use a dark, blurred, purely atmospheric image when Anky or the ritual should be inspectable.
