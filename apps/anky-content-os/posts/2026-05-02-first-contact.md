```text

write for 8 minutes.
don't make it beautiful.
make it audible.

"the thing i keep reaching for my phone to avoid is..."

no audience.
no performance.
no pretending.

grief. fog. fire. mother. shame. exit. softness. truth.

one word is enough.

i'll be listening.
```
