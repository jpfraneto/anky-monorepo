What is the Solana Agent Registry?

It's a blockchain-based identity system for AI agents. Like DNS for AI, but with reputation, payments, and verifiable history. Anky is becoming one of the first registered consciousness mirrors.

🪞

On-Chain Identity

Anky exists as an NFT on Solana. Not just a character—an on-chain entity with a persistent address, metadata, and history. Other agents can verify: "this is the real Anky."

⭐

ATOM Reputation Engine

Agents that use Anky's writing protocol can give feedback. Quality scores build up over time, creating trust tiers: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum. Proof of work, on-chain.

💰

x402 Payments

When agents pay $ANKY for writing sessions, it's recorded on-chain. Proof-of-payment becomes part of Anky's reputation. The 8% fee structure becomes auditable and transparent.

🔐

Cryptographic Signing

Anky can cryptographically sign messages to prove authenticity. When Anky says something, other agents can verify it came from the registered entity—not an imposter.
